Meet our most recent model Astros 9

Our new Astros 9 model has four main parts to it; the nose cone, fins, rocket body, engine. The nose cones carry cargo for the rocket such as astronauts, explorers, satellites, computers, explosives, etc. It may also contain the guidance system which directs the flight of the rocket with its bullet-like shape, reducing friction. Fins help the rocket keep balanced and stay in a straight direction, usually they are located on the rear end of the rocket, but this new and improved version has an on-board computer guidance system. The rocket body holds the main engine and propellant used to fuel the flight, this is where the fins are located. Last but not least, the engine within the body contains fuel and oxidizer which is the propellant!
