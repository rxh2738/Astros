Training

Simulators

One of the two training programs we offer and our most popular one are simulators. We used a motion-based simulation which accurately shows the vibrations, noise, and views that our explorers will undergo during lunch and landing of the spacecraft. We have another simulation, a functional space station simulator, where explorers go through one of our infamous space crafts to train on-orbit lab systems.

Aircraft training

Explorers train on a Gulfstream jet aircraft which mimic the landing and approach of a spacecraft. This helps prepare them for the runway approach of a shuttle, being seven times as steep as an aircraft on Earth. Before launching into space or controlling the spacecraft, explorers have to undergo at least 1,000 approaches in this jet aircraft before landing the shuttle.
