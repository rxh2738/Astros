    Meet Our Team

    Rocket Scientists

    At Astros, our rocket scientists are behind the scenes to your embarking of space. They are the foundation of our company, designing our rockets to ensure your utmost safety and success in space. They are all certified with a masters/PHD background in aerospace engineering, in which many have proficiency in specific skills, such as, aerodynamics, mechanical/software engineering, mathematics, and physics. As we are a team, these scientists also create flight-specific training software for astronauts to use whilst training the future generation of explorers in space.

    Astronauts

    To ensure safety on the way to the many wonders of the universe, our astronauts stay on field and direct training before launch. One of our most popular training programs uses our microgravity flight simulator, where our astronauts familiarize our explorers with complex vehicles, suits, equipment, launch/landing and protocols. They also conduct spacewalks, robotic tasks, aircraft training (with over 1,000 approaches). Explorers acquire up to a total of 300 hours of training before their first mission.

    Stations

    One of our many stops to the final destination of any wonder of the universe are our Astro space stations. In our space stations, you can see our on-sight astronauts, rocket scientists, and other team members doing what they do in real time. One of our many on-sight astronauts will be your escort, presenting to you the most wonderful of the universe, our space stations. These tours feature the equipment, experiment, and many computers offered at the docking port. It's always exciting to see how an explorer turns into a team member.
